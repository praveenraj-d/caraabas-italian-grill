WHAT IS A CARRABBAMICO?

Being part of our family is a big responsibility and we know it takes a special kind of person to take care of people the way we do – you have to feel it in your heart and soul. It's not the easiest job but if you have what it takes, it will be the best. When you join the family, you will be immediately welcomed as a Carrabbamico (aka "Mico").
